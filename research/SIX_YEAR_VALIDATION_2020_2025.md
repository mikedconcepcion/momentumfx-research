## Statistical Validation

### Chi-Square Goodness-of-Fit Test

**Null Hypothesis (H₀)**: Zone formation is uniformly distributed across time (no OB preference)
**Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)**: Zones concentrate during OB windows

**Expected Distribution** (if null hypothesis true):
- OB windows: 33.3% of zones (130 × 0.333 = 43.3 zones)
- Non-OB time: 66.7% of zones (130 × 0.667 = 86.7 zones)

**Observed Distribution**:
- OB windows: 111 zones (85.4%)
- Non-OB time: 19 zones (14.6%)

**Chi-Square Calculation**:
```
χ² = Σ((Observed - Expected)² / Expected)
χ² = ((111 - 43.3)² / 43.3) + ((19 - 86.7)² / 86.7)
χ² = 105.77 + 53.01
χ² = 158.78
```

**Critical Value** (α = 0.001, df = 1): 10.83

**Result**: χ² = 158.78 >> 10.83
**Conclusion**: **REJECT null hypothesis with p < 0.001**

The pattern is **highly statistically significant**. The probability of this distribution occurring by chance is less than 0.1%.
